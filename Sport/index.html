HOW WILL MY PROJECT WORK ?

The goal is to help training centers to detect the best athletes from the youngest age.

Collecting Data

We will start by collecting 100m sprinter data over the last 10 years.

Data storage

We are going to set up a specific database in order to put our data in an organized way.

Creating an AI

We are going to create an AI able to compare the data and use them.

Data processing with AI

The AI will retrieve the basic data, thanks to an algorithm that will compare and create statistics on the performance of young athletes.

Athlete detection

The algorithm will then select the athletes that it believes have the potential for.

Athlete Selection

The AI will then make a selection on all the young people who are out of the batch after the passage of the algorithm.


The database

First of all we are going to set up a FireBase database.


We will then manually enter the performances that we have achieved over several years.


The data are now organized in a way that will allow our AI to retrieve them easily and succeed in highlighting what we want.

The Artificial Intelligence

Artificial intelligence will be used to process the data retrieved from the database.


The development of AI will consist in taking data, analyzing them, comparing them with the data of young athletes.


The algorithm implemented will therefore make a first selection on the multitude of young people who come to make a tryout, which will greatly reduce the work of the coaches.


He will then be able to choose between the different young athletes selected in order to keep only the required number at the beginning.


It is finally to the role of the coach who will be able to judge the remaining young people, the detection algorithm allows him to save a lot of time and to judge only promising young people.
